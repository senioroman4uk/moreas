Production

System of raw materials quality control
Quality control system at the plant starts with the quality control of incoming raw materials.
Quality control of incoming raw materials begins when unloading.
At our enterprise commission with modern laboratory equipment works, which makes the sampling of fish and seafood for the study of physico-chemical and microbiological parameters. A card of incoming raw materials with a full description of organoleptic characteristics is compiled, as well as with the type of packaging, the type of cutting, weight characteristics, labeling, controlled by the presence and complying with all relevant supporting documents.
Raw materials, which at least one of the indicators does not meet the requirements will not be allowed for the preparation of products.

 

Workshop for the production of tinned fish

Raw material before packing into tins (and while and after packing) is subjected to heat treatment. In this group, the following types of fish preserves are singled out.

Preserves in tomato sauce are produced from various species of fish, pre-cut, dried, blanched or roasted. Prepared and packed in tins semi-finished product is poured with tomato sauce, tins are rolled and sterilized. Certain kinds of tinned preserves in tomato sauce are produced from raw materials without prior heat treatment with pouring into it concentrated tomato sauce. Preserves in tomato sauce are used as a cold staticetizer.

Preserves in vegetable oil are produced from different species of fish, pre-cut, smoked, roasted or blanched. Semi-finished product, packed in tins is  poured with a high-quality vegetable oil or a mixture of oils, tins are rolled and sterilized. Preserves in vegetable oil  are used as a snack.

Fish and vegetables preserves are produced from a variety of fish and sturgeon milt with adding vegetables (onion, carrot, cabbage, pepper, chilli etc.).
Fish is pre-fried or blanched, vegetables are packed fresh or dry, rarely - in roasted. At present fish and vegetables preserves are produced in a wide range in the form of fried fish with vegetables, cabbage rolls, meatballs with the addition of various vegetable toppings, tomato sauce, butter, broths and hot savory fillings. These kinds of preserves are used in the form of cold staticetizers, as well as semi-finished products for the preparation of the first and second courses.

Organic preserves are produced from pre-cut fish with a juicy and delicious meat. Cut fish is packed usually without heat treatment, adding salt. However, in the preservation of some species of fish spices, oil, gelling broth are sometimes added. Organic preserves have a high nutritional value, they are used for first and second courses, cold staticetizers and salads.
